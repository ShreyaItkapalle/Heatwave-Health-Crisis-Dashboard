# 🌡️ Heatwave & Health Crisis Dashboard
### Analysing India's Summer Heat Stroke Impact (2015–2024)
**Microsoft Elevate | AICTE Internship | Data Analyst Track | Feb 2026 Batch**

---

## 📊 Project Overview
A 5-page interactive Power BI dashboard exposing India's heatstroke 
health crisis, government under-reporting, and economic impact 
across 10 high-risk states from 2015 to 2024.

## 🔑 Key Findings
- 17,000+ heatstroke cases across 10 states
- Only 39.7% of deaths officially reported (6,000 hidden deaths)
- Bihar has lowest preparedness score (55/100) and highest hidden deaths
- Peak power demand surged 59% from 2015 to 2024
- Every 10-point rise in preparedness = 15% fewer deaths
